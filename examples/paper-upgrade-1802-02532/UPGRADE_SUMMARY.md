## Breakthrough Thesis

The strongest 2026 paper is not a cleaned-up rerun of the 2018 benchmark story. It is a new computation-backed interface paper arguing that serialization is a first-class interface for irregular 3D and biomolecular learning, with the 2018 Hilbert-mapping paper reinterpreted as an early precursor to that now-active design space. The new ingredient is an executable context-budget probe that makes the interface claim measurable.
