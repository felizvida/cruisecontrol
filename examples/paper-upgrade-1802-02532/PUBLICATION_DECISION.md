# Publication Decision

**Source paper**: arXiv:1802.02532  
**Decision**: `PIVOT + EXECUTABLE PROBE`

## Why Not A Direct Revision

A direct empirical resubmission of the 2018 paper would still be weak in 2026 because:

1. the baseline set is historically outdated
2. the empirical scope is too small for a modern full methods paper
3. the strongest enduring contribution is the serialization idea, not the original benchmark table

## Why The Workflow Continues

A real publication-grade upgrade still exists, but it is a different paper:

### New paper type

Hybrid literature-synthesis / executable-interface paper

### New core thesis

Serialization is a first-class interface between irregular 3D structures and sequence-native model families, and the 2018 Hilbert-style mapping paper can be reinterpreted as an early precursor to this now-important design space.

### Minimum non-cosmetic delta

- New claim set: yes
- New evidence base: yes, modern literature plus a new deterministic context-budget probe
- New publication story: yes

## Outcome

Proceed to a new paper, not a facelift, and strengthen the pivot with real executable computation rather than cosmetic rhetoric.
